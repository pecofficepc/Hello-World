NEWS FLASH: We have 335 pre-registered for camp!!

Texas Camp #4 has been in operation since Pine Spring Baptist Camp was opened back in the 1970's. It was the fourth Texas camp organized and thus the name "Texas Camp #4." It is a Christian camp and campers are required to attend all of the worship services and to obey the rules.  

All children attending this camp must have an adult who will be responsible for them. You must furnish a minimum of one female adult per 10 girls and one male adult per 8 boys. Children under 8 years of age must be accompanied by their parent or gardian.

In 2018 the cost per camper is $95.00. Click the link below to register Online. Click on the link to the left to download the camp brochure which contains all the information you need for camp. The schedule of activities is in this brochure. Print out as many as you need for your campers.

NEW BACKGROUND CHECK RULES AS OF 2018

All those who are 18 years and older must pass a Criminal background check and have taken and passed the child abuse prevention course. As of 2018 you must provide a sexual misconduct background check as well. Each church is responsible to provide proof of these background checks and course completion.
